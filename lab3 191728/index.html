Culinary Arts Institute

Welcome to Culinary Arts Institute, our esteemed International Culinary Arts School situated in Westlands in the city of Nairobi, Kenya. As a leading Catering Training establishment, we take pride in our distinguished reputation and commitment to Culinary Excellence. Culinary Arts is the art of preparing, cooking, and presenting food. Explore different cuisines and become a master chef!

Courses Offered

  • Basic Cooking Skills: A hands-on introduction to kitchen tools, ingredients, and fundamental cooking methods. Learn knife skills, food prep, boiling, sautéing, grilling, and how to follow professional recipes with confidence.
  • Pastry & Baking: Dive into the sweet world of baking! Master dough preparation, cake decoration, artisan bread, croissants, puff pastry, and chocolate artistry under expert guidance.
  • International Cuisine: Travel the world without leaving the kitchen. Prepare authentic dishes from Italy, China, Mexico, France, India, and more. Understand ingredients, cooking traditions, and plating styles from different cultures.
  • Food Safety & Hygiene: A vital course for all culinary professionals. Learn global food safety standards, cross-contamination prevention, personal hygiene, and safe food storage and handling practices.
  • Advanced Culinary Techniques: Take your skills to the next level with training in sous-vide, molecular gastronomy, butchery, fermentation, and creative food design.
  • Professional Chef Certification: A comprehensive, career-focused program that combines all core skills into a professional certification—ideal for aspiring executive chefs and entrepreneurs.

Our Services

At Culinary Arts Institute, we provide more than just classes—we offer a gateway into the global culinary scene:

  • Personalized Cooking Classes: Private lessons tailored to your interests, from beginner-level guidance to expert techniques in your preferred cuisine.
  • Group Workshops and Seminars: Fun and interactive group sessions perfect for birthdays, team-building, or school enrichment programs.
  • Catering Services for Events: Impress your guests with beautifully crafted menus for weddings, corporate functions, and private parties—all prepared by our talented students and chefs.
  • Recipe Development and Food Consulting: Let our experts help you create standout recipes, menu engineering strategies, or improve kitchen operations for your business.
  • Food Photography and Plating Training: Learn how to style food like a pro and capture high-quality visuals that make your meals shine on menus, websites, and social media.
  • Internship and Job Placement Assistance: Connect with top hotels, restaurants, and cruise lines for real-world experience and career growth.
